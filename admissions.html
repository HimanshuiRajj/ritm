How to Apply

  1. Visit the official RITM admissions portal or our campus.
  2. Fill the application form with all required personal and academic details.
  3. Upload necessary documents including marksheets, certificates, and photographs.
  4. Submit the application and pay the registration fee.
  5. Attend counseling or entrance tests (if applicable).
  6. Receive confirmation and further instructions from RITM.
